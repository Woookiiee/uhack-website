About US!

Because we value your life ❤️

Our technoloy platform connects several nearby rescue agencies, hospitals and police stations and also share the location of the victim at the time of calamity so that necessary aid can be provided to those who are in need.
Users just have to register themselves on our platform and press the SOS button at the time of disaster so that our services can reach and save them.
On the other hand,there are rescue agencies,hospitals and police stations connected with eachother and the user.They will recieve an emergency message along with the location of the user who is in danger and can rescue them.


Our Vision is to save as many lives as we can at the time of calamity. Beacuse life is precious and we value you.
